Philosophy of Medicine

"No longer a compassionate healer working at the bedside and using heart and hands as well as mind, the physician has become an impersonal white-gowned ministrant who works in an office or laboratory. Too many physician no longer learn from their patients, only from their professors.

- Robert Becker, MD - The Body Electric

Though it is a bit dreamy to remember house calls from a physician who looks after his trade as an artisan would over his or her craft, I wonder if most of us still prefer the precision of modern medicine over the humanitarian ethos of pre-modern practices. If I had to guess I think we would like both! Human innovation is perhaps inevitable, and to deliberately reject is in a sense the antithesis of being human, but progress for the sake of progress is what we call cancer. Our Services

Comprehensive Consultation

Delve deep into your health journey with our 60-90 minute personalized consultation, available in-person or via video conference. We take the time to understand your past experiences, address your current challenges, and establish your future health goals. Our thorough evaluation includes a review of your current prescriptions and an exploration of potential alternative approaches. If necessary, we can order bloodwork during the consultation to gain further insights into your health. To ensure continuity of care and progress, this service includes two additional 20-minute follow-up visits. Priced at $350, invest in a truly customized approach to your health and well-being.

Efficient Quick Visits

Address acute illnesses or medication refills with our convenient Quick Visit service, designed to provide timely and effective care. Priced at just $75.00, these visits offer a focused solution for your immediate health concerns without the need for a full consultation. Experience prompt, professional attention that respects your time and prioritizes your well-being.

Flexible Membership Programs

Embrace comprehensive health support with our tailor-made Membership Programs, designed to accommodate your unique care needs. Our memberships include an initial full consultation, unlimited follow-up visits, and access to quick visits for acute concerns. Enjoy the convenience of text, call, or in-person consultations, ensuring seamless communication with your healthcare provider. Prices range from $50 to $500 per member per month, depending on your individual care requirements. With no contracts required, you can experience personalized, ongoing support that prioritizes flexibility and adaptability to your ever-changing health journey.

Dr. Chamberlain is a dedicated functional medicine physician and the proud founder of his private practice in Austin, Texas. With a strong background in family medicine and emergency care, he discovered his true passion for functional medicine and has since been committed to helping his patients achieve optimal health through a holistic and preventative approach.

Dr. Chamberlain's unique blend of experience enables him to delve deep into the root causes of health issues, addressing them with a personalized and integrative approach. His practice emphasizes the importance of nutrition, lifestyle, and mental well-being, empowering patients to take control of their health and thrive.

Outside of his professional life, Dr. Chamberlain is an avid traveler, constantly seeking new experiences and broadening his understanding of health and wellness from diverse cultural perspectives. He also enjoys staying active and maintaining a balanced lifestyle by engaging in regular workouts and exploring the great outdoors in beautiful Texas.

Dr. Chamberlain invites you to join him on a transformative journey towards achieving vibrant health and well-being, guided by his extensive expertise and the core principles of functional medicine.
