Patient Portal

Convenient and Caring

Text, call or email anytime! One of my goals is to make primary care as streamline and easy as possible. After working in busy clinics and hospitals with long waits and ugly art, I decided this was not the way forward. Office workers like to joke about a 50 minute meeting that could have been a 5 minute email. I felt the same way about a large majority of clinic visits. I want the easy stuff to be easy so the tougher situations can be taken seriously.

Our Services

Comprehensive Evaluation

Let’s deep dive into your health journey with a 60 minute personalized consultation, available in-person or remote conference. I’d like to understand your past experiences, address your current challenges, and establish your future health goals. We will review your current prescriptions and explore potential alternative approaches. Includes two 20-minute follow up visits.

Efficient Quick Visits

Address acute illnesses or medication refills quickly and conveniently. Members can text or call anytime for acute care concerns. New patients will need to set up consultation or introductory visits.

Flexible Membership Programs

Let me be your ‘pocket doctor.’ Text or call anytime. Read something weird online? Send me a screenshot and I’ll give you my thoughts. Want to check some labs you read about? Send me a message and let's order them. Prices may vary based on health history.

About Me

Dr Chamberlain obtained his undergraduate degree at Texas State University. He obtained a BS in Biology. In undergraduate he tutored math, science, and volunteered with the Catholic Student Center and helped out at a local hospital. For medical school he attended Texas College of Osteopathic Medicine in Fort Worth, TX. He then attended residency in Family Medicine at the Dell Seton Med Center in Austin, Texas.

Since graduating from residency, Dr Chamberlain has worked in a variety of practice settings including private outpatient, rural clinical practice, urgent care, occupational health, emergency room and hospital/inpatient settings. Dr Chamberlain has extensive primary care experience and brings a unique perspective to solving long standing difficulties.

Dr Chamberlain is interested in helping people with addictions, ADD/ADHD symptoms, depression/anxiety, metabolic disorders such hypertension, diabetes, dyslipidemia as well as a host of other chronic conditions. He understands that some people will need medications to assist with these problems depending on severity, but he believes with whole hearted enthusiasm that even long standing chronic conditions can be reversed under the right conditions.

Dr Chamberlain is a devout follower of Christ, and is an active member of St Mary’s Cathedral in Austin, TX. He enjoys running, lifting weights, playing piano and spending time with family and friends. He holds an active license with the Texas Medical Board, is a board certified member of the American Board of Family Medicine, and is currently pursuing a certificate in functional medicine hosted by the Institute of Functional Medicine.
